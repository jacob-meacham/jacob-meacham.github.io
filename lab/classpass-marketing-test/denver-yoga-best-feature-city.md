# The best yoga studios in Denver/Boulder

From the Front Range foothills to the heart of the city, the Denver/Boulder area is home to an incredible community of yoga studios offering everything from powerful heated flows to candlelit restorative sessions. We analyzed hundreds of real ClassPass member reviews across dozens of studios to find where the best yoga experiences in Denver and Boulder actually happen -- so you can roll out your mat somewhere you'll love.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback from the ClassPass community.

## 1. Golden Hour Yoga -- Sunrise Vinyasa Flow

Golden Hour Yoga lives up to its name. Their Sunrise Vinyasa Flow class holds a perfect 5.0 rating, and members consistently describe it as the ideal way to start the day. The studio combines breathwork, meditation, and lengthening postures into a practice that's welcoming for yogis at every level. Whether you're flowing through their sunrise gentle class or a more dynamic vinyasa, the instructors here create an experience that's both grounding and energizing.

> "Excellent, moderately paced class in a cozy studio. Really nice way to start the day."

> "The instructor was so nice and taught such a lovely class!"

**Rating:** 5.0 (43 reviews)

## 2. Yoga Center of Denver -- Gentle Somatic Yoga

Yoga Center of Denver is a longtime anchor of the city's yoga scene, and their Gentle Somatic Yoga class is a standout. Through slow, deliberate movements that foster deep bodily awareness, this class helps you release tension, improve flexibility, and reconnect with your body. Members praise the warm, welcoming atmosphere from the front desk all the way through to the final pose.

> "Safe, peaceful, and restorative. From the front desk to the instructor, everyone was kind and welcoming. Can't wait to go back."

**Rating:** 4.97 (89 reviews)

## 3. 12th House Yoga -- Solar Flow + Yin

12th House Yoga has quickly become a Denver favorite, and their Solar Flow + Yin class perfectly balances mindful movement with quiet surrender. Members love the studio's focus on breath-to-movement connection and the variety of flows offered, including yin and hatha. The instructors are known for knowledgeable hands-on assists and clear verbal cues that make every practitioner feel supported.

> "Easily my favorite studio in Denver. They focus on breath to movement and offer many types of flows, including yin. The instructors are fantastic, super knowledgeable and helpful with hands-on assists and verbal cues. Highly recommend!"

**Rating:** 4.97 (34 reviews)

## 4. Sankalpa Pole Fitness -- Maitri Movement Yoga

Don't let the studio name fool you -- Sankalpa's Maitri Movement Yoga class is one of the most beloved yoga experiences in the Denver area. This intimate, playful practice is designed to help you feel nourished, centered, and embodied through mindful movement, breath, and reflection. Reviewers describe it as unlike anything else they've experienced, and the energy of the instruction keeps members coming back again and again.

> "One of my favorite instructors ever. There is nothing like these classes. Incredible energy -- I always walk away feeling renewed. Good for beginners and seasoned yogis alike."

**Rating:** 4.95 (42 reviews)

## 5. Santosha Studios -- Candlelight Restore

Santosha Studios offers a candlelit restorative yoga experience that members describe as the real deal. Bolsters, blankets, and sand bags are provided, and the instructor takes time to help each person maximize comfort and benefit in every posture. If you're looking for a deeply relaxing practice that goes beyond a simple stretch, this is the class to try.

> "If you are looking for real restorative yoga, this is it! Bolsters, blankets, and sand bags are provided, and the instructor expertly helps each person use them to maximize the comfort and benefits of the postures. Highly recommend!"

> "The class was amazing, well paced, and very welcoming. The instructor was super helpful and clear."

**Rating:** 4.79 (52 reviews)

## 6. Yoga Box -- HOT YB

Yoga Box's Capitol Hill location brings the heat -- literally. Their HOT YB class is set between 98 and 105 degrees, creating an environment designed to melt away everyday stress while building strength and flexibility through a dynamic flow. Members love the clear cueing, the meditative atmosphere, and the feeling of total reconnection with their bodies.

> "Great class with super motivating instruction. Loved the flow and the heat. The instructor's cues were clear and easy to follow -- will be back!"

> "Enjoyed my first OG hot class in a long time. Felt reconnected with my body and enjoyed the cues and guidance through the flow."

**Rating:** 4.80 (59 reviews)

## 7. Sunny Isle Yoga -- Yin Yang

Sunny Isle Yoga in Arvada brings a warm, homey atmosphere to every class, and their Yin Yang practice is a member favorite with over 150 reviews. This class strikes a perfect balance between energizing flow and restorative stillness, leaving you in a calm, centered state. The instructors are praised for their patience, fantastic pacing, and ability to create a genuine sense of community.

> "Beautiful space, very homey and comfortable ambience! Great sense of community within the group and wonderful leader. I loved this perfect balance of flow and restore."

# The best strength training studios in Atlanta

We looked at over 1,100 reviews across six strength training studios to find where Atlanta's best strength training experiences happen. CrossFit Adrenaline earns a perfect 5.0 average rating, making it Atlanta's highest-rated strength training class. The Training Box in Roswell has built a loyal following with 236 reviews and a 4.89 average rating. Riverside EpiFitness Club in Austell offers a Total Body class that blends bootcamp-style conditioning with full-body strength work, earning a 4.89 average rating across 96 reviews. Fitness Marietta's ROWFIT class is one of the most popular strength training options in the Atlanta area, with over 500 reviews and a 4.88 average rating. The class combines rowing, kettlebells, dumbbells, and medicine balls in a high-intensity interval format that keeps your heart rate elevated throughout. Reviewers love the welcoming atmosphere and the coaches who make every experience feel personal.

> "It was incredible. It was a perfect class to get me back into it -- challenging, but very doable. Everyone there was so kind and welcoming."

## 5. F45 Training -- T10

F45 Training in Buckhead brings a team-driven circuit format to strength training, with their T10 class earning a 4.86 average rating across 169 reviews. The studio runs multiple strength and HIIT formats, so you'll never get bored. With multiple coaches on the floor correcting form and offering encouragement, you get a personal-training feel in a group setting.

> "My first time here and I will be returning. There were three instructors for about 10 people, so they were very involved in correcting form and encouragement. It was a difficult class and I was definitely fatigued by the end."

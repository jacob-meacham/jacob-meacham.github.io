# The best cycling studios in Austin

Austin's indoor cycling scene is booming -- from rhythm-driven rides with concert-level energy to power-focused sessions that mix cycling with strength and cardio. We analyzed over 1,800 reviews across seven studios to find where the ClassPass community clips in and keeps coming back. Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback.

## 1. SoulCycle -- SoulSurvivor and SOUL X BILT

SoulCycle's downtown Austin location has built one of the city's most passionate cycling communities. Their SoulSurvivor class is the highest-rated cycling experience in Austin, and members consistently describe it as more than just a class -- it's an emotional and physical experience wrapped into one. Instructors like Chris and Paige bring energy that fills the room, and regulars show up as much for the community as for the ride.

> "Chris is one of a kind special. I treat his class as an extension of therapy. The joy you will find with his leadership is unmatched. The community he attracts is also so kind and welcoming."

> "My first ever cycling class here was awesome -- challenging but fun. Paige is great at pumping up the energy and welcoming new people."

**Rating:** 4.96 (500+ reviews) | SOUL X BILT: 4.91 (98 reviews)

## 2. Motiv Fitness Austin -- 30 | 30 (Cycle | Barre)

Motiv Fitness Austin offers a unique hybrid class that pairs 30 minutes of cycling with 30 minutes of barre, giving you the best of both worlds in a single session. With 500+ reviews and a near-perfect rating, this is one of the most consistently loved cycling experiences in the city. Members rave about the instructor lineup, the sound system, and a clean, well-maintained space with plenty of free garage parking nearby.

> "Olivia's energy is off the charts! She teaches an awesome class and I love that she's super in touch with everyone in the room. Playlist was great, and I had so much fun!"

> "I love all of the instructors here, but Carl is quickly becoming one of my favorites! Great energy, great playlists, and the class is always challenging. He also offers modifications which as a beginner I really appreciate."

**Rating:** 4.96 (500+ reviews)

## 3. CycleBar -- Empower (45 min)

CycleBar in Cedar Park delivers a polished cycling experience with a personal touch. From the moment you walk in, the staff goes above and beyond -- members frequently mention the decorated lockers, free shoe rentals, and cold towels at the end of class. The Empower class blends endurance and intensity over 45 minutes with themed rides that keep things fresh, and the comfortable seats mean you can push hard without dreading the next day.

> "This hip-hop class was so fun. The music was great, the vibes were there, and the staff and instructor were so friendly, kind, and welcoming. I will be back!"

> "This place is extremely nice! Everyone was very friendly, and they even decorated the locker they picked out for me. The class was fun and the seats were very comfortable."

**Rating:** 4.90 (500+ reviews)

## 4. Truecore Fitness -- TrueRide and Rhythm Ride

Truecore Fitness in Leander has carved out a loyal following with their lineup of themed cycling classes. TrueRide anchors the schedule with a solid indoor cycling foundation, while the Rhythm Ride series brings creative energy with themed sessions spanning pop punk, movie soundtracks, and more. Dimmed lights, cool towels at the end, and a staff that helps first-timers get set up on the bike make this a standout spot for Austin-area riders of all levels.

> "This was the best cycling class I have ever taken. It was challenging and fun, and the instructor was encouraging and energizing."

> "Truecore Fitness has an amazing team! The friendly staff helped me set up the bike and made sure I was comfortable from the start. The entire experience was so welcoming."

**Rating:** 4.89 (91 reviews) | Rhythm Ride: 5.0 (18 reviews)

## 5. TruFusion -- 45 Min. Tru Ride (Indoor Cycling)

TruFusion in South Austin brings the heat -- literally -- to their indoor cycling classes. Their Tru Ride series includes themed sessions and a hip-hop ride that members love for its high energy and great playlists. The instructors are attentive and engaging, making this a strong choice for anyone who wants a cycling experience that feels more like a party than a class.

> "Loved the class -- the instructor is a wonderful teacher! Great playlist and awesome energy."

**Rating:** 4.80 (35 reviews) | Hip Hop Ride: 4.90 (10 reviews)

## 6. Castle Hill Fitness -- CYCLE Classic

Castle Hill Fitness is an Austin institution, and their CYCLE Classic class brings a more traditional cycling experience with quality equipment and polished amenities. The studio uses Keiser indoor bikes known for engaging your core, and the facility offers towels and a sauna to help you recover after class. It's a great option if you're looking for a clean, well-equipped space with a straightforward cycling format.

> "The studio is nice, super clean, and has towels and a sauna in the locker room. The Keiser indoor bike works your core more than other types -- it's a quality cycling experience."

**Rating:** 4.76 (93 reviews)

## 7. Wild Basin Fitness -- Power Cycle

Wild Basin Fitness rounds out the list with a cycling class that emphasizes terrain simulation and instructor attention. Power Cycle takes you through rolling hills, steep climbs, fast flats, and bumpy roads, all set to motivating music. Members highlight the instructor's focus on proper form and her energy that keeps you moving even when the ride gets tough.

> "Sharon's energy is wonderful -- it keeps you going and working hard. It was a nice sweat, great exercises, and I enjoyed the music."

> "Such a fun class! The instructor takes moments during class to correct everyone's form, which is so important. I loved that attention to detail."

**Rating:** 4.72 (29 reviews)

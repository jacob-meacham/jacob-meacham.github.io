# The best strength training studios in Washington, DC

Washington, DC is a city that runs on energy -- and that extends well beyond Capitol Hill. We looked at thousands of reviews across dozens of strength training studios to find where the DC area's most dedicated and enthusiastic community members are building strength, pushing limits, and coming back for more. Whether you're chasing a kettlebell class in the District or a barbell-based experience in the suburbs, these studios earned top marks from the people who actually showed up.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback from the ClassPass community.

## 1. MADabolic -- WORK. REST. REPEAT.

MADabolic in Arlington has built a reputation as one of the most consistently praised strength training studios in the Washington, DC area. Their signature WORK. REST. REPEAT. class carries a 4.90 average rating across 500+ reviews -- a remarkable feat at that volume. The structured interval format (30 seconds on, 30 seconds off across two circuits of five stations) keeps every class predictable enough to plan around and intense enough to challenge you at any level.

> "Fun and challenging class. Lots of options to go at your own weight, but the pace is set so you know what's coming."

**Rating:** 4.90 (500+ reviews)

## 2. WeRep Fitness -- Kettlebells

WeRep Fitness brings serious energy to its Kettlebells class, and the community has noticed -- 234 reviews and a 4.91 average rating speak for themselves. Members rave about the immaculate space, the extensive equipment, and instructors who know how to keep the energy high with great music and hands-on coaching. First-timers consistently report feeling safe and welcomed.

> "The whole studio is immaculate, the equipment selection is extensive, and the music was great. I can't recommend this studio enough!"

> "Really great class! Amazing vibes and very welcoming. I got great instructions to do things safely for the first time. The exercises were dynamic enough to keep things interesting, and the time went by fast."

**Rating:** 4.91 (234 reviews)

With 281 reviews and a 4.78 average rating, this studio has carved out a niche for members who want to build strength as an integrated, whole-body practice.

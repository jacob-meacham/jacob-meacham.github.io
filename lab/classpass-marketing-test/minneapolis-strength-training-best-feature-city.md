# The best strength training studios in Minneapolis/St. Paul

From high-energy circuit training in Eagan to functional fitness in Highland Park, the Twin Cities area has built one of the most dynamic strength training scenes in the Midwest. We looked at thousands of reviews across dozens of studios to find where Minneapolis and St. Paul's best strength training experiences happen. Whether you're drawn to HIIT-style circuits, classic CrossFit, or yoga-infused sculpt classes, these studios consistently earn rave reviews from the ClassPass community.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback.

The screen-guided format makes it easy to follow along, while coaches circulate the room offering encouragement and form corrections that make every rep count.

# The best dance studios in Washington, DC

Washington, DC's dance scene stretches well beyond the Beltway, and the ClassPass community has been paying attention. We looked at over 1,400 reviews across the region's top dance studios to find where DC's best dance experiences happen. From pole fitness and salsa to Afro-Brazilian movement and ballet, these classes earned their spots through consistently glowing feedback and the kind of energy that keeps members booking again and again.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback across the ClassPass community.

This Zumba class at Simon Says Yoga has become one of Washington, DC's most beloved dance experiences, holding a near-perfect 4.98 average across 208 reviews. Whether you're progressing through inversions or refining your flow, the supportive atmosphere makes all the difference.

**Rating:** 4.98 (133 reviews)

It's a welcoming entry point for anyone curious about salsa, tango, or Latin dance in general.

> "The studio is spacious, the instructor has great energy, and they also have dance socials every Saturday -- a perfect opportunity to practice what you learn."

**Rating:** 4.94 (80 reviews)

Afro Brazilian Dance DC offers something truly unique in the Washington, DC dance landscape. My Body Shop in Riverdale holds a perfect 5.0 average rating for their Exotic Tease class across 36 reviews, making it one of the highest-rated dance experiences in the DC area. Reviewers love the way the instructor breaks down choreography step by step, creating space for beginners to feel confident while still challenging more experienced dancers. The studio itself is welcoming and spacious, and the overall vibe is empowering and fun.

Metropolitan School of the Arts brings classical dance training to the Washington, DC community with their Ballet 1 - Beginner class, which holds a 4.91 rating across 53 reviews.

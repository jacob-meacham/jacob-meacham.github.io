# The best yoga studios in San Diego

San Diego's yoga scene is as diverse as its coastline -- from rooftop domes overlooking La Jolla to outdoor decks steps from the ocean in Ocean Beach. We analyzed over 4,800 verified ClassPass reviews across the greater San Diego area and found a collection of studios that consistently deliver standout experiences. These are the places where the community shows up again and again, drawn by exceptional instruction, creative class formats, and spaces that feel as restorative as the practice itself.

Studios on this list were selected based on review volume, average rating, and the consistency of enthusiastic feedback from the ClassPass community. We prioritized studios where reviewers highlighted knowledgeable instructors, welcoming atmospheres, and classes that meet practitioners at every level.

## 1. Prana Yoga Center -- Vinyasa, restorative, and inversions

Located in the heart of La Jolla, Prana Yoga Center is a San Diego institution with an impressively deep class lineup -- from sunrise vinyasa flows and hatha fundamentals to candlelight restorative sessions paired with sound bath savasanas. What sets Prana apart is range: reviewers describe a studio where you can challenge yourself with handstands and inversions one day, then melt into a deeply calming restorative class the next. Instructors here earn consistent praise for hands-on adjustments, thoughtful guidance, and the ability to create an experience that feels both challenging and accessible.

> "This is my favorite yoga place in La Jolla. The instructor helped guide and made adjustments -- it was awesome. And she had a great playlist!"

**Rating:** 4.96 (473 reviews for Handstands & Inversions) | 4.93 (345 reviews for Sunrise Vinyasa Yoga)

## 2. Riffs Yoga Studios -- Restorative yoga with aromatherapy

Riffs Yoga Studios offers one of the most unique yoga experiences in San Diego. Their signature Restorative Yoga with Aromatherapy class pairs gentle, supported poses with essential oil blends, creating a practice designed to realign and refresh both mind and body. Reviewers rave about the studio's striking setting, the quality of the oils, and the depth of knowledge the instructors bring to each class. With complimentary tea and a warm, friendly staff, Riffs turns every visit into something that feels like a retreat.

> "Absolutely stunning studio. Whimsical outdoor setting, complimentary tea, friendly staff. The oils used were potent and the teacher was well-informed."

**Rating:** 4.91 (500+ reviews)

## 3. Soul Of Yoga -- Roll & Restore

Based in Encinitas, Soul Of Yoga is a beloved studio known for its deeply restorative approach. Their Roll & Restore class -- which incorporates therapy balls, bolsters, and guided release techniques -- has become a community favorite for anyone seeking recovery and renewal. Reviewers describe instructors who bring profound, layered teaching with genuine warmth, and a studio atmosphere that feels special from the moment you walk in. It's the kind of place where you leave feeling noticeably different than when you arrived.

> "What an amazing experience. The classes are always so special and profound -- absolutely phenomenal."

**Rating:** 4.94 (422 reviews)

## 4. Namaste Fitness -- Ashtanga and vinyasa

Namaste Fitness in Chula Vista brings serious craft to traditional yoga styles. Their Ashtanga Yoga class stands out for instructors who actively help practitioners deepen their poses, offering personalized cues and hands-on guidance that reviewers describe as both empowering and fun. The studio itself is well designed, and the class experience balances athletic challenge with a playful energy that keeps people coming back. Whether you're exploring ashtanga for the first time or building on years of practice, Namaste delivers.

> "The instructor really helped me get deeper into the poses and was just overall good energy!"

**Rating:** 4.95 (314 reviews for Ashtanga Yoga) | 4.85 (211 reviews for Vinyasa I)

## 5. Pier To Point Yoga and Wellness -- Aerial restorative flow

Pier To Point Yoga and Wellness offers one of San Diego's most distinctive yoga experiences: aerial restorative flow classes held on a beautiful outdoor deck. Their Aerial Slow & Low Restorative Flow combines the gentle, supported quality of restorative yoga with the creative freedom of aerial silks, and the outdoor setting adds a layer of calm that's hard to replicate indoors. Reviewers consistently describe this as a one-of-a-kind class that feels both exciting and deeply relaxing.

> "Amazing class and amazing instructor! Loved it!"

**Rating:** 4.89 (267 reviews)

## 6. Trilogy Sanctuary -- Vinyasa and slow flow

Perched on a rooftop in La Jolla's village, Trilogy Sanctuary is a yoga studio, vegan cafe, and community gathering place all in one. Yoga classes here take place in an iconic outdoor dome and on the sky deck, creating an inside-outside experience that reviewers find unforgettable. Instructors bring deep knowledge of both the physical and spiritual sides of yoga, and the welcoming atmosphere makes everyone -- from first-timers to seasoned practitioners -- feel at home. The cafe downstairs is a bonus that turns a single class into a full morning ritual.

> "This was my first time at this place and it was wonderful. I love how it's at the rooftop and in the fresh air. The instructor was super friendly and welcoming, plus she helped a lot with adjustments. Great class!"

**Rating:** 4.88 (82 reviews for Vinyasa Flow 2) | 5.0 (19 reviews for Slow Flow + Sound Healing)

## 7. Yoga Box -- Power flow vinyasa

Yoga Box in Ocean Beach delivers a heated power vinyasa experience that reviewers describe as both meditative and intense. With the temperature set between 96 and 98 degrees, the class is designed to build internal heat, challenge your strength, and then bring you back down with deep stretching and a satisfying cool-down. What stands out in reviews is the quality of instruction -- clear, precise cueing that lets you stay fully immersed in the flow without needing to open your eyes.

> "Great teacher, very clear instructions -- I never even had to open my eyes. The flow was amazing."

**Rating:** 4.95 (42 reviews)

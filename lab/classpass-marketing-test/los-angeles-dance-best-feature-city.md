# The best dance studios in Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a city that moves to its own rhythm. From the stages of Hollywood to the social dance floors of the Eastside, dance is woven into the fabric of this city. Whether you are a total beginner looking to try your first class or a seasoned mover searching for a new studio, LA and Orange County deliver some of the best dance instruction anywhere in the country.

We looked at thousands of ClassPass member ratings and reviews to find the dance studios that consistently earn rave feedback. These are the spots where the music is great, the instructors are world-class, and every newcomer feels welcome from the moment they walk in.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback from the ClassPass community.

Ballroom Dance Academy in Los Angeles is the highest-rated dance venue in the city, and it is not hard to see why. With two flagship classes -- Salsa Level II and Argentine Tango -- this studio draws dancers of every background into a warm, inclusive community. Instructors have built a loyal following by combining expert technique with an atmosphere that feels more like a party than a lesson.

> "The instructors are amazing!! 10/10! I highly recommend this class to anyone who has ever thought about taking a salsa class. You won't regret it."

> "10/10. Beautifully diverse class and easy to follow. Gets you out of your comfort zone in a safe and welcoming community."

**Rating:** 4.96 (500+ reviews on Salsa) | 4.94 (169 reviews on Argentine Tango)

## 2. ATOMIC Ballroom -- Line Dancing 1

ATOMIC Ballroom in Irvine is a dance destination that keeps surprising people. With a wide range of partner and social dance classes -- from line dancing and swing to waltz and tango -- this studio is beloved for its relaxed, beginner-friendly energy. The private class space, rotating partners, and encouraging instructors make it a standout for anyone stepping onto the dance floor for the first time.

> "Cool vibe, super nice people, fun class! Definitely beginner-friendly, so go for it. The space is private for the class, and it's comfortable and welcoming!"

**Rating:** 4.93 (131 reviews)

## 3. Precision Dance Company -- Zumba

Precision Dance Company in Lomita has turned Zumba into something special. Under expert guidance, classes here go beyond a typical cardio workout and lean into real dance -- pulling from Latin, Afro, and pop styles to create choreography that is exciting and accessible at the same time. The supportive community atmosphere keeps members coming back week after week.

> "Victoria was a wonderful instructor and the environment was very supportive. The choreography was different than other Zumba classes I've taken in the past -- more on the dance side versus workout -- but I found it really exciting and fun!"

**Rating:** 4.92 (329 reviews)

## 4. Ballet Bodies -- Ballet Beginner

Ballet Bodies in Los Angeles has become the go-to studio for adults who want to experience the beauty and discipline of ballet without years of training. Members consistently praise the ability to break down complex combinations, provide personal corrections, and create an atmosphere where everyone from total beginners to experienced dancers feels challenged and supported.

> "Romi is incredible! Her classes are a great workout and she walks you through every combo. I love her mix of classic and contemporary music, it's always a great atmosphere."

**Rating:** 4.87 (142 reviews)

## 5. Diaz Studio of Dance -- Jazz Technique (Adult)

Diaz Studio of Dance in Culver City holds the rare distinction of a perfect 5.0 average rating. This intimate studio focuses on jazz technique for adults, blending classic dance training with a modern, high-energy approach. Members love the combination of dance and conditioning work, and the welcoming vibe that makes every class feel like an event.

> "Fun class, good energy -- a great mix of dance and weights. I will be back!"

**Rating:** 5.0 (44 reviews)

## 6. LA Bellydance Academy -- Shimmy Clinic

LA Bellydance Academy in Los Angeles offers something you will not find at most fitness studios: the art of bellydance taught with depth, history, and serious technique. The instructor is praised for graceful demonstrations, detailed breakdowns, and the way she connects movement to the cultural roots of the dance. The studio houses a boutique, and the community of dancers here is one of the most welcoming in the city.

> "Sarah is an amazing intro teacher -- great pacing, fun choreography that is the same each week so we get to see progress, and just so alluring in her stage presence. Highly recommend for first timers."

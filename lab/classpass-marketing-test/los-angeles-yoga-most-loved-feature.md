# Most loved yoga in Los Angeles

From candlelit hot studios in Huntington Beach to peaceful Pasadena sanctuaries, Los Angeles offers a yoga landscape as vast and varied as the city itself. We dug into thousands of community reviews to surface the studios that inspire the deepest loyalty — the ones members call "my favorite" and return to week after week.

Studios were ranked by review enthusiasm, average rating, and the consistency of superlative praise from the ClassPass community.

## 1. Yoga Tribe — Hot Yoga Candlelight

Yoga Tribe in Huntington Beach has cultivated a near-reverent following among Orange County yogis. The studio's traditional approach to hot yoga, complete with candlelit ambiance, earns glowing review after glowing review. Members describe the experience as both deeply authentic and genuinely welcoming.

> "One of the best teachers I've ever had. She has so much knowledge about the practice and shares it in every class. Very traditional, very inspiring — she offers modifications and is super aware of each person."

> "Loved everything about this class. The spacing between people was great even though it was full. The instructor, the vibe, the intentions — it's my favorite hot yoga studio in Orange County."

**Rating:** 4.94 (341 reviews)

## 2. Round Willow Yoga — Vinyasa All Levels

Manhattan Beach's Round Willow Yoga has built a community of devoted practitioners who credit the studio's instructors with transforming their practice. Members consistently highlight the personal attention and thoughtful sequencing that make every class feel both challenging and restorative.

> "Her classes always quiet my mind and ease my soul. She brings so much peace but always manages to sneak in a laugh. Truly one of my favorite instructors in my six-plus years of yoga practice."

> "Clean and relaxing space. The instructor didn't hesitate to correct poses or posture, which I appreciate. A great class I'd definitely come back to."

**Rating:** 4.88 (321 reviews)

## 3. Greenheart Yoga — Beginners' Yoga Blend

Pasadena's Greenheart Yoga holds a special place in the community for its deeply personal approach. Members consistently praise the studio's patient, injury-aware teaching style and the beautiful, prop-rich practice space that supports practitioners at every level.

> "The instructor was extremely understanding, listened to me explain my injuries, and tailored the class accordingly. She was knowledgeable, patient, and had such a soothing demeanor. The new studio is brand new and beautiful."

**Rating:** 4.86 (448 reviews)

## 4. Yoga Bliss — Prenatal Yoga

Yoga Bliss in Westchester has earned an outpouring of love from its community, with members describing the studio owner's warmth and attentiveness as genuinely memorable. The welcoming atmosphere extends to every class, but the prenatal program draws especially devoted praise.

> "The owner amazingly remembered me after more than five years since my last visit, which shows how much she genuinely cares about her students. Her class is beautifully guided with a calming voice that creates such a peaceful atmosphere."

> "Loved the community aspect of the class with other first-time moms. Will definitely come regularly throughout my pregnancy."

**Rating:** 4.92 (129 reviews)

## 5. The Wellness of OZ — Gentle Flow

Tucked away in Burbank, The Wellness of OZ offers an experience reviewers describe as a genuine escape from the city. The intimate, unpretentious setting and thoughtful instruction have earned the studio a loyal following among members seeking connection and calm.

> "This is a hole in the wall and such an amazing disconnect. The instructor was incredibly kind and supportive."

> "A really great sound healer who genuinely cares about your needs."

**Rating:** 4.87 (46 reviews)

## 6. Yoga Vibe — All Levels Flow

In the heart of Los Feliz, Yoga Vibe delivers consistently creative, high-quality classes that keep members coming back. Reviewers love the studio's approachable energy and the instructors' ability to make every class feel fresh.

> "Really enjoyed the class — creative and engaging. A space where you feel welcome from the moment you walk in."

**Rating:** 4.90 (50 reviews)

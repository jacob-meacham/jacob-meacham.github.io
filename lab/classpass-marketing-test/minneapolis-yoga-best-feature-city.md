# The best yoga studios in Minneapolis

Minneapolis and St. Paul are home to a thriving yoga community -- from heated flows and aromatherapy-infused sessions to holistic hatha and creative vinyasa, the Twin Cities offer something for every practitioner. We analyzed over 920 reviews across six studios to find the yoga experiences that keep the ClassPass community coming back to their mats, and these six rose to the top.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback from the ClassPass community.

## 1. Yoga Sanctuary -- Vinyasa Yoga and Holistic Hatha

Yoga Sanctuary in Minneapolis is one of the most beloved yoga destinations in the Twin Cities, with over 400 reviews across its vinyasa, hatha, and flow classes. Members describe the space as warm, cozy, and full of good energy -- a place where instructors create creative, well-paced flows that challenge your balance while honoring your body's needs. Whether you're deepening a hatha practice or flowing through a vigorous vinyasa, the teaching here is knowledgeable, attentive, and deeply personal.

> "What a sweet, good-energy space! The instructor made up a really fun and creative flow that kept us listening, challenged our balance, and had a nice progression. All around lovely experience."

> "The yoga foundations class was great for new and trained yogis alike. The step-by-step breakdown of the poses and how to make the most of each stretch made the experience feel very intentional. A great way to start the day slowly while still getting a boost of energy."

**Rating:** 4.94 (423 reviews)

## 2. Platinum Yoga -- Yoga Flow for All Levels

Platinum Yoga in Burnsville has earned one of the highest average ratings of any yoga class in the Minneapolis area. Their Yoga Flow for All Levels lives up to its name -- members consistently highlight how welcoming the space feels, from the instruction to the fellow practitioners on the mat next to you. The class strikes a balance between challenging and relaxing that keeps people coming back week after week.

> "The whole class was very welcoming, challenging, and relaxing! Can't wait to go back."

> "I've been to a lot of yoga classes in my life and this was the most welcoming class I've attended. The instructor and fellow yogis were friendly and warm. Suitable for all levels."

**Rating:** 4.92 (117 reviews)

## 3. Open Minds Fusion Studio -- EARTH GODDESS Yoga

Open Minds Fusion Studio in Minneapolis offers one of the most unique yoga experiences in the Twin Cities with their EARTH GODDESS Yoga class. This creative blend of power yoga, dance movement, and heart-centered meditation has built a passionate following. Members describe the energy as unlike anything else -- a class where intuitive movement, mudra, and mantra come together in a flow that's easy to follow yet deeply transformative.

> "Such a fun class. Not traditional yoga but a great combo of dance and yoga. Definitely incorporates challenging flows and poses to expand your practice."

> "The best class I have ever found on ClassPass. The energy is like no other -- I felt like I made a new group of friends immediately in this community, and I can't wait to come back."

## 4. azItala -- Hot Yoga

azItala in Elk River is a top destination for hot yoga in the Minneapolis area, offering everything from beginner-friendly heated sessions to aromatherapy-enhanced Oils and Asana classes. The heated room and slow, guided movements create an experience that members describe as deeply restorative and safe. Instructors are welcoming and kind, guiding you through each pose with clear verbal cues and a variety of modifications.

> "Very calming and relaxing class. The instructor was incredible and made me feel very welcomed."

> "The hot room and slow guided movements were very relaxing and restful. The instructor was very welcoming and kind. The space felt very safe and restorative."

**Rating:** 4.92 (198 reviews)

## 5. C4 Fitness -- Yoga

C4 Fitness in Apple Valley brings an encouraging, high-energy approach to yoga that members love. Their yoga class focuses on detoxifying postures, shoulder and hip openers, and building mobility and focus -- all in a supportive atmosphere where the instructors know every participant by effort, not just by name. Members appreciate that each week brings a different flow, keeping things fresh and engaging.

> "The instructor is great -- very welcoming to newcomers and beginners while motivating regulars to push a little harder. Each class is different every week so it doesn't get boring."

> "The instructor paid close attention to each participant's performance, offering corrections when needed and always giving words of encouragement. The positive energy and attention to detail made the experience very enjoyable."

**Rating:** 4.80 (153 reviews)

## 6. The Power House -- Yoga

The Power House in Highland Park, Saint Paul, rounds out this list with a versatile yoga class that members describe as a wonderful way to create balance and calm. The studio has built a welcoming and fun environment where instructors emphasize going at your own pace and focusing on proper form. It's the kind of community where you feel supported whether you're just getting started or have been practicing for years.

> "Very welcoming and fun environment! The instructor emphasized going at your own pace and focusing on proper form."

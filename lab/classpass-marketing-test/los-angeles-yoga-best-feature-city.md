# The best yoga studios in Los Angeles

Los Angeles is a city that lives and breathes yoga -- from beachside vinyasa flows in Manhattan Beach to heated studios in the heart of Hollywood. After analyzing over 9,600 verified ClassPass reviews across the greater Los Angeles and Orange County area, a clear group of standout yoga studios emerged. These are the places where the community keeps coming back, class after class, drawn by thoughtful instruction, welcoming spaces, and classes that leave people feeling genuinely transformed.

Every studio on this list was selected based on consistently high ratings and detailed, enthusiastic feedback from the ClassPass community. We looked at overall rating averages, volume of reviews, and the specific language people used to describe their experiences -- prioritizing studios where attendees highlighted knowledgeable instruction, inclusive atmospheres, and classes that met practitioners at every level.

## 1. Kinship Yoga -- Hot Yoga & Yin

Kinship Yoga has built a devoted following in Los Angeles with a class lineup that spans energizing hot flows to deeply restorative yin and sound bath experiences. Reviewers consistently praise the studio's warm, inclusive atmosphere and its instructors' ability to break down complex poses in ways that feel accessible to everyone. With amenities like a steam room, cold plunges, hot tea, and alkaline water, the experience extends well beyond the mat -- making every visit feel like a full reset.

> "LOVED this class. In a beautiful room with skylights and a wonderful instructor. A nice repose from more aggressive heated flows, this class was the best way to start a day and the teacher assisted us during savasana which was so relaxing. A new favorite."

**Rating:** 4.95 (500+ reviews)

## 2. Round Willow Yoga -- Vinyasa & Power

Nestled in Manhattan Beach, Round Willow Yoga is a community favorite for vinyasa and power yoga classes that balance challenge with intention. Reviewers describe an environment where instructors create genuinely welcoming, lighthearted spaces while still pushing practitioners to grow. The studio draws people in with its charm -- a cozy, beautifully designed space that feels like a neighborhood gem -- and keeps them coming back with consistently excellent instruction and thoughtful modifications for all levels.

> "Gaia created such a welcoming and safe environment! She is genuinely friendly, knowledgeable, and created a very unique and fun sequence. The space is so cute too! Highly recommend."

**Rating:** 4.93 (500+ reviews)

## 3. Greenheart Yoga -- Vinyasa Flow

Located in Pasadena, Greenheart Yoga stands out for its dedication to alignment-focused, detail-oriented instruction. Reviewers love that instructors here actively correct positioning and offer hands-on guidance, making it an especially rewarding studio for those who want to deepen their understanding of each pose. The bright, airy studio space feels warm and inviting, and the community that gathers here values authenticity and heart-centered teaching -- qualities that come through in every class.

> "Wonderful instructor, class, and studio. The instructor was very welcoming and I appreciated how she guided the practice with such wisdom and nurturing support. The class itself was a good balance between strength and mobility. The studio space felt bright and airy."

**Rating:** 4.94 (500+ reviews)

## 4. YogaSix Redondo Beach -- Slow Flow & Power

YogaSix Redondo Beach offers a diverse menu of yoga styles -- from slow, meditative flows to intense heated power classes -- all under one roof. What the ClassPass community loves most is the range: whether you want a gentle, restorative experience or a sweat-drenched challenge, there is a class that fits. Reviewers frequently highlight the welcoming energy of the instructors, the clean and well-maintained space, and how each class feels thoughtfully sequenced from warm-up to cool-down.

> "Sara's class is absolutely amazing! The energy she brings as an instructor is incredible -- her vibe is so welcoming and uplifting. I felt so strong and supported throughout. The flow was perfect, and the entire space felt like a safe and positive environment."

**Rating:** 4.90 (500+ reviews)

## 5. The Wellness of OZ -- Gentle Flow & Yin

Tucked away in Burbank, The Wellness of OZ is the kind of studio that reviewers describe as a hidden treasure -- a small, intimate space where every class feels personal. With gentle flows, yin yoga, and a meditative approach to movement, this studio is ideal for anyone looking to slow down and reconnect. Reviewers highlight the personalized attention from instructors, the use of essential oils and calming music, and a class experience that feels more like a retreat than a workout.

> "Absolutely loved it. The class was small and we got to contribute to the type of class it would be based off of our unique needs. Instructions were clear and demonstrated well -- it was a good mix of athletic challenge while still being deeply meditative."

**Rating:** 4.87 (46 reviews)

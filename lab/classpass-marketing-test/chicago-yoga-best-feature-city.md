# The best yoga studios in Chicago

Looking for the perfect yoga class in Chicago? Whether you are chasing a powerful hot vinyasa flow or a grounding restorative session, the Windy City has a thriving yoga scene with studios that deliver world-class instruction, welcoming communities, and spaces designed to help you find your center. We rounded up the top-rated yoga studios in Chicago based on thousands of real ClassPass member reviews so you can step onto your mat with confidence.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback from the ClassPass community.

Whether you are deepening an existing practice or looking to be challenged in fresh ways, this studio delivers.

# The best yoga studios in Washington, DC

From gentle flows in Silver Spring to heated vinyasa in the heart of DC, the Washington area's yoga scene is as diverse as the city itself. We looked at over 2,000 reviews across the region's top-rated studios to find where the ClassPass community's most rewarding yoga experiences happen. Whether you're looking for a calming yin practice after a long workday or an energizing morning flow to start your day right, these are the studios that consistently earn the highest marks.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback from the ClassPass community.

Grace Studios in Silver Spring has earned one of the highest yoga ratings in the Washington, DC area, and reviewers are quick to explain why. Members love how the instructors break down both familiar and more complex poses with clear descriptions, making the class genuinely approachable for all levels. Whether you're brand new to yoga or returning after a break, the pacing here meets you where you are.

> "I woke up looking for a class to learn new movements. The instructor showed and described the more complex poses really well, and I appreciated how approachable it all felt. I'll definitely be coming back regularly."

The Fitness Equation in Ashburn brings a well-rounded yoga experience to the Washington, DC area, with over 500 reviews and a 4.79 average. ## 4. Epic Yoga DC -- Warm Honey Yin

Epic Yoga DC has become a go-to destination for yogis across Washington, backed by over 500 reviews and a 4.67 average rating. The Warm Honey Yin class is designed to improve mobility and flexibility through longer, more intentional holds, and reviewers consistently describe the experience as deeply restorative. The studio's peaceful atmosphere and instructors who encourage mindful form over ego earn high praise across the board.

> "This is the best yoga class I've been to in a long time. The instructor encouraged us to focus on form rather than feeding our ego. It was just what I needed -- a little work but not too much."

**Rating:** 4.67 (500+ reviews)

Gold's Gym locations across the Washington, DC area have quietly built a strong reputation for yoga, with 273 combined reviews spanning Hatha, Vinyasa Flow, and other styles.

# The best yoga studios in Atlanta

Atlanta's yoga scene is as vibrant and diverse as the city itself, with studios spanning from the Westside to Midtown to the northern suburbs offering everything from heated power flows to meditative yin sessions. We analyzed over 1,200 real ClassPass member reviews across Atlanta's top yoga studios to find the classes that earn the most consistently glowing feedback -- whether you're looking for a challenging sculpt class or a slow, restorative wind-down.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback from the ClassPass community.

# The best dance studios in New York

New York's dance scene is as diverse as the city itself, and the ClassPass community has spoken. We looked at over 1,300 reviews across New York's top dance studios to find where the city's best dance experiences happen. From hip-hop choreography to Latin social dance and cardio-fueled routines, these classes earned their spots through consistently glowing feedback.

Studios were selected based on review volume, average rating, and consistency of positive feedback across the ClassPass community.

## 1. Mind Your Body -- Hip Hop

With a near-perfect 4.98 average rating across 200 reviews, the Hip Hop class at Mind Your Body is one of New York's most celebrated dance experiences. Reviewers highlight the challenging routines, the infectious energy, and the way every class doubles as a serious cardio session. Whether you're a seasoned dancer or just looking for a fun way to move, this one keeps the community coming back week after week.

> "I look forward to this class every Monday night. The routines are challenging but so much fun, and it's a great cardio experience."

**Rating:** 4.98 (200 reviews)

## 2. Mind Your Body -- Jazz On Heels

If you're looking for something that blends artistry with attitude, Jazz On Heels at Mind Your Body delivers. This class holds a perfect 5.0 rating from 27 reviews, and members describe the choreography as world-class. The welcoming atmosphere and the instructor's ability to make every dancer feel like a star set this class apart from anything else in the city.

> "The instructor's talent, expertise, and incredibly artful choreography make this class something special. The welcoming dancers and classmates keep me coming back."

**Rating:** 5.0 (27 reviews)

## 3. Crunch Gym -- Hard Knocks

Hard Knocks at Crunch Gym on West 54th Street is a high-octane cardio dance class that has built a devoted following, with 345 reviews and a 4.85 average rating. Members love the nonstop energy, the creative choreography, and the way the class lets you lose yourself in the music. It's the kind of experience where you forget you're working out because you're having too much fun.

> "High energy from start to finish -- nonstop moving with really cool choreography. Such a fun way to lose yourself in the music."

**Rating:** 4.85 (345 reviews)

## 4. Arthur Murray -- Newcomer Dance Class

Arthur Murray's Newcomer Dance Class is New York's most-reviewed dance offering on ClassPass, with 449 reviews and a 4.76 average rating. The class introduces beginners to foxtrot, tango, waltz, and more in a supportive, partner-friendly environment. Reviewers praise the attentive instructors and the way the class balances structured technique with pure enjoyment, making it ideal for anyone exploring dance for the first time.

> "A lovely introduction to basic dance steps in foxtrot, tango, and waltz. The instructors shared helpful pointers to make the steps more elegant -- crucial for a beginner who needs guidance while keeping track of the steps. I'd highly recommend this to anyone exploring dance."

**Rating:** 4.76 (449 reviews)

## 5. Arthur Murray -- Salsa

Salsa at Arthur Murray carries a 4.82 rating across 113 reviews, and members consistently highlight the vibrant, social atmosphere. The instructors bring passion and patience in equal measure, and the class structure makes it easy for newcomers to jump in without a partner. Reviewers describe the experience as energizing, fun, and a genuine taste of New York's Latin dance culture.

> "Such a fun environment. The salsa instruction was amazing -- you can feel the love for dancing and the authenticity in every class."

**Rating:** 4.82 (113 reviews)

## 6. Limitless Fitness -- Zumba

The Zumba class at Limitless Fitness in Queens brings together great music, thoughtful choreography, and a genuine sense of community. With a 4.83 rating across 40 reviews, members praise the instructor's energy and the way each class balances functional movement with dance-driven fun. Reviewers also note the friendly, communal vibe among participants, making it a welcoming space for all levels.

> "The instructor keeps it fun and moving, and you genuinely get a great experience. There's a real sense of community within the class -- the people are friendly, and it's wonderful to see."

**Rating:** 4.83 (40 reviews)
